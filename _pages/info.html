Zinezilla is BACK with a vengeance, returning to the Bristol's Trinity Centre following last year's buzzing celebration of everything creative the South West has to offer!



Zinezilla Arts Fair
Featuring Animinspo Animation Festival
Saturday 12th September 2026, 11:30am - 6:30pm
Trinity Centre, Bristol

Downstairs you'll find a comic, illustration and zine fair bursting at the seams with ziney and zillary goodness from over 50 artists to feed your eyes and souls.

Jaunt upstairs for animation screenings and discussions hosted by our wonderful friends at Animinspo (https://www.animinspo.com/), who will once again be awarding the best in independent animation.

Rounding out the day there'll be a variety of workshops, talks and performances to take part in and enjoy. A full programme will be released later.


Keep an eye out for the Zinezilla Comics Anthology that will be making a return, as well as our big kaiju friends Gordon, Kong and Meg who you'll find dotted around the festival.

Applications to table at the fair are closed.

Applications to screen films at Animinspo can be found here: https://filmfreeway.com/ANIMINSPO
