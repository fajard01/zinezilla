Trinity Community Arts Centre
Trinity Rd, St Jude's
Bristol BS2 0NW

trinitybristol.org.uk

Accessibility
The venue is accessible, with lift access to the first floor and accessible toilets located on both floors. There are four designated accessible parking bays in our 24hr Pay & Display car park, located by the daytime reception - left side of building. All visitors are required to Pay & Display, including Blue Badge Holders. There is free on street parking available for Blue Badge Holders within a 50yd radius of Trinity including areas of Stapleton Road and Old Market.

By Public Transport
Trinity is a 20 min walk, or 15 min bus (number 8), from Temple Meads train station. It is also a 20 min walk or 20 min bus ride from Bristol Bus and Coach station. The centre is serviced by many buses running down Old Market and Stapleton Road.

By Car
There is a small car park managed by Car Flow and an ANPR system and charges apply for all people parking at Trinity, including Blue Badge holders. There is space for drop-off /pick-up access. There is no parking on the road outside Trinity but there is some street parking nearby. The venue is situated around a one-way system along with roads only accessible to buses so please plan your journey.
